# Marketing Writing Examples

## Example 1 — Landing page hero
**Prompt:** Write hero copy for a privacy-focused note-taking app.

**Output:**
**Your ideas stay yours.**  
Capture notes, plans, and passwords in one encrypted workspace built for people who do not want their private thinking turned into someone else's data.  
Trusted by teams that treat confidentiality as infrastructure.  
**CTA:** Start secure

## Why it works
- Strong outcome-first headline.
- Clear value proposition.
- Credibility line supports the CTA.

---

## Example 2 — Short ad copy
**Prompt:** Write 3 paid ad variants for a meal-planning app.

**Output:**
1. Stop asking what’s for dinner. Plan a week in 5 minutes. Start free.
2. Less stress. Fewer grocery runs. Smarter meal plans for busy households.
3. Turn random ingredients into real dinners. Open the app and cook tonight.

## Why it works
- Each line targets a pain point.
- The copy is compact and channel-ready.
- The CTA is implied or explicit.

---

## Example 3 — B2B email
**Prompt:** Write a short outbound email for scheduling software.

**Output:**
Subject: Cut booking friction for your sales team

Hi Elena,  
Your team should not be losing meetings because of back-and-forth scheduling. Our platform lets prospects book qualified demo slots directly into rep calendars, with routing rules, reminders, and no manual chasing.

Teams use it to reduce no-shows and move leads faster from interest to conversation.

Open to a 15-minute walkthrough next week?

## Why it works
- Clear business problem.
- Practical value, not generic hype.
- CTA is specific and low-friction.

---

## Example 4 — Product description
**Prompt:** Write product copy for a compact desk lamp.

**Output:**
A small lamp for serious work. It casts even, adjustable light without taking over your desk, and the rotating head lets you direct brightness where you need it most. Ideal for reading, sketching, and late-night focus.

## Why it works
- Product benefit is immediate.
- Physical features serve real use cases.
- The tone is clean and purchasable.
